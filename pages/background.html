Project Background & Motivation

Traditional classroom instruction often struggles to maintain engagement, especially in subjects like science and math where abstract concepts can feel disconnected from daily experience. Game-based learning offers a way to bridge that gap by providing immediate feedback, a sense of progress, and hands-on problem solving.

This project builds on that idea by developing an educational 2D dungeon-crawler that integrates curriculum-aligned puzzles into an interactive environment. The motivation stems from the belief that learning should be both challenging and enjoyable — an experience where curiosity and persistence are naturally rewarded.
